About this tool

A search and browsing interface for OCR-transcribed Ottoman Turkish printed works. Page images are shown with their detected regions and lines; the transcriptions are searchable across one or all works.

How to use

  • Choose a work from the Work menu, then scroll the pages (or click a thumbnail / use ↑ ↓).
  • Search any character, word, or phrase. Use regex for patterns (e.g. قر.ن), and normalize to ignore ye/kef variants and ZWNJ.
  • Scope the search to all works or one work, and to all regions or one region type.
  • ❒ Browse lists every instance of the chosen region type (e.g. all footnotes or all images) across the works in scope, with a cropped thumbnail and a count for the whole corpus and per work. Step through them with ↑ / ↓ and click any to open it on the page.
  • Step through matches with ↑ / ↓ (or Enter / Shift+Enter); matched lines are highlighted on the page.
  • Toggle the regions and lines overlays. Click a line to read its transcription; hover for a tooltip.
  • The ⌨ keyboard types Ottoman-Turkish letters into the search box. Fit fits the page to the window.
